Fake News Detector

Photo by Pixabay

This app predicts whether a political news article on the 2016 US election is real or fake.

The model was trained on ~70,000 news articles from 3 separate labeled sources (University of Virginia, University of Tennesse, and Kaggle). While a definite time period is ambigiuous across most of the sources, the topic of the articles are primarily around the 2016 US election.
